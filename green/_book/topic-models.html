6.1 topic representation

different topic representations take different approaches to assigning topic labels. the two I have used, in addition the default approach, are (1) the KeyBERTInspired topic representation and (2) the MaximalMarginalRelevance topic representation.

KeyBERT (default): assigns topic labels based on most distinguishing words in the topic (similar to ip-idf).

KeyBERTInspired: does something extra, still trying to understand.

MaximalMarginalRelevance: reduces redundancy of selected topic keywords (e.g., car vs. cars)

OpenAI (trying to get this to work): supplies ChatGBT with a representative selection of topic documents and keywords and asks it to return a 5-10 word topic label.
